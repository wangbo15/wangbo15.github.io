I am an Assistant Professor (equivalent to Lecturer in China) in School of Computer Science and Technology at Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU). I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from Peking University in 2021. I was a visiting Ph.D. student at the National University of Singapore (2019) and a visiting scholar at Singapore Management University (2024).

My recent research projects are mainly focused on:
1) Compiler Testing and Tuning (TSE 2025);
2) Mutation Analysis (ASE 2021, ISSTA 2017);
3) Automated Program Repair (TOSEM 2022, TOSEM 2020);
3) AI4SE.
