I am an Assistant Professor (Lecturer Equivalence) in School of Computer and Information Technology at Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU). I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from Peking University in 2021. I was a visiting Ph.D. student at National University of Singapore (2019).

My recent research projects are mainly focused on:
1) Mutation Analysis (ASE 2021, ISSTA 2017);
2) Automated Program Repair (TOSEM 2022, TOSEM 2020);
3) The intersection of AI and system software testing techniques.
